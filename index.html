Hi, I am Debodyuti Biswas, CSE UG Student at SRM Institute of Science and Technology.

I am a self-taught UX/UI designer with 1+ year of experience. I specialise in creating creative user intefaces and maintaining design systems for web and mobile applications and softwares in Figma.
