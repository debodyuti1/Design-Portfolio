About

More About Me.

Hi, I am Debodyuti Biswas, CSE UG Student at SRM Institute of Science and Technology.

I am a self-taught UX/UI designer with 1+ year of experience. I specialise in creating creative user intefaces and maintaining design systems for web and mobile applications and softwares in Figma. With the basic knowledge of HTML, CSS, and Javascript, I am able to understand how layouts are built and implemented. Added to this, I have also taught myself graphic design which helps me create amazing designs for my interfaces and presentation. Thus, I can own every step of the process, from ideation and user research to design hand-off and prototyping.

Lately I've also taken on the mission to share everything I know with the design community via my Instagram. Feel free to join me on this mission of becoming a better designer.
